Explore student jobs most useful to Math, CS, Data Science, and Cybersecurity majors

Overview

On-campus jobs offer Mount St. Mary's students opportunities to gain real-world experience, earn income, and develop professional skills. These jobs are flexible with class schedules and accessible through the University's HR department.

Jobs Related to Math and Computer Science

  • Information Technology Service Center (ITSC)
  • Learning Services Peer Tutor (for Math or CS courses)
  • Laptop Note-taker (in technical or CS-heavy classes)
  • Writing Center Tutor (for technical writing or coding-based documentation)
  • Science Lab Aide (in tech or programming-related labs)
  • Admissions Telecounselor or Phonathon Caller (build communication skills valuable in tech roles)

Application Process

Job postings are available on the MSMU Human Resources website. Navigate to Employment Opportunities and then Student Employment. Most listings go live in late July, with hiring through mid-September or until filled. Some jobs require informal interviews.

Requirements & Eligibility

  • Open to students with Federal Work Study or University Employment funding
  • Under 18? Apply for a Maryland Work Permit
  • All students must complete Workday onboarding and I-9 document verification
  • Work limited to 8 hours/week (unless exempt in certain departments)
  • Students are paid Maryland's minimum wage every two weeks via direct deposit
