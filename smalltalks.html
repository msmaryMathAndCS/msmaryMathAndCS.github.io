Learn more about the School of Science, Mathematics & Technology

What is a Smalltalk

    A Smalltalk is a 30-45 minute presentation on topics related to Computer Science, Cybersecurity, Data Science, or Mathematics.

    These topics are researched by students and presented to anyone among students or faculty that would like to attend.

    This program was started by Dr. Portier in 2008 before being taken over by Dr. Heinold as the coordinator in 2009.

Source: Personal Interview with Dr. Heinold

How to get Involved:

Requirements to Present:

  • Propose a topic to Dr.Heinold that relates to Mathematics, Computer Science, Data Science, or Cybersecurity.

  • Research your topic

  • Create a cohesive presentation to discuss the topic

  • Schedule a time with Dr. Heinold to present the topic in Lauglin Auditorium
